Who We Are

The Light and Nature Foundation (LNF) is a community-rooted, nonprofit organization based in Kisumu, Kenya. We exist to uplift the lives of marginalized populations while nurturing the natural world around us. By fusing human dignity with environmental consciousness, we inspire renewal, equity, and sustainable living for all.

Vision Statement

To illuminate a world where the harmony between light and nature inspires renewal, awareness, and sustainable living for all life on earth.
